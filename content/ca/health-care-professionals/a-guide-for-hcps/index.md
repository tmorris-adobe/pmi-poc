# A guide for HCPs

CBD & Medical cannabis

CBD and medical cannabis in general may be new to many healthcare professionals (HCPs), so our aim is to provide you with reliable information and guidance to help you make informed decisions for your patients. As such, this page includes some of the most frequently asked questions about CBD and medical cannabis that HCPs ask when exploring it as an option.
