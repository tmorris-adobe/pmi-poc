# What ingredients are in Luo CBD Lozenges?

Luo CBD Lozenges contain the following ingredients: Mannitol, Vegetable oil (Hydrogenated coconut oil, Hydrogenated palm kernel oil, Palm kernel oil), Magnesium stearate, Cannabidiol isolate, Silicon dioxide, Acesulfame Potassium, Sucralose, Brilliant Blue FCF, Natural flavours.
