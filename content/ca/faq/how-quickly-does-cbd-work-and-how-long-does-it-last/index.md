# How quickly does CBD work? And how long does it last?

The speed and duration of the effects of medical cannabis can vary depending on the method of administration. The table below provides a guide:

| Type of medical cannabis | How quickly effects are felt | How long effects last |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Inhalation (smoking or vaporizing) | 5-10 minutes | 2-4 hours |
| Oromucosal spray | 15-45 minutes | 6-8 hours |
| Oral consumption | 1-3 hours | 6-8 hours |
