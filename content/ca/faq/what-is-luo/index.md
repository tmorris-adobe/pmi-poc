# What are Luo CBD lozenges?

Luo is a high-quality oral lozenge made with CBD isolate, which is a highly concentrated form of CBD.

Each bottle contains 30 lozenges and is available in vanilla mint flavour. Luo's oral lozenges are discreet, portable and easy to use.
