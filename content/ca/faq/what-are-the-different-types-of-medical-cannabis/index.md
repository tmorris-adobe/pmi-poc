# What are the different types of medical cannabis?

The most common types of medical cannabis used are:

- Inhalation
    - Smoking (burning the flower)
    - Vaporization (heating concentrated solutions or heating the flower)
- Oral
    - Oil solutions
    - Oral solutions
    - Capsules
    - Edibles
    - Tablets
- Rectal/vaginal suppositories
- Oral spray
- Muscular injection
- Topical application
