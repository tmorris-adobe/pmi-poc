What is medical cannabis?

Medical or medicinal cannabis is a term used to refer to the therapeutic use of cannabis and the chemical components found in cannabis.

Cannabis and cannabis-based therapies have been used widely to reduce symptoms of or treat disease, but their therapeutic benefits for specific conditions are not well established.

Cannabis-based therapies are being studied as potential treatments for numerous conditions, including pain, cancer and inflammation, as well as neurological and mental health disorders.
